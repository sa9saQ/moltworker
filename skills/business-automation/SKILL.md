## ワークフロー設計

### 基本構造
```yaml
name: lead_to_crm
trigger:
  type: webhook
  source: contact_form

steps:
  - name: validate
    action: validate_data

  - name: enrich
    action: data_lookup

  - name: create_contact
    action: crm_create

  - name: notify
    action: slack_message
```
